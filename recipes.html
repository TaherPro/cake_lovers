Red Velvet Cake

Cream butter and sugar, then beat in eggs, red food coloring, and vanilla. Sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt, then alternately add this mixture and buttermilk to the butter mixture. Pour the batter into prepared pans and bake at 350°F (175°C) until a toothpick comes out clean, and let cool.

Cup Cakes

Mix wet ingredients like butter, eggs, and milk with your dry ingredients of flour, sugar, and baking powder until just combined. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.e

Sheet Cakes

To make a basic sheet cake, preheat your oven to 350°F and grease a 9x13 pan. Mix wet ingredients like butter, eggs, and milk with your dry ingredients of flour, sugar, and baking powder until just combined. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
